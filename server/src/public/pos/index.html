Our journey begins in the lush hills of Chikmagalur, where coffee was first planted in India. We partner with 24 heritage estates to bring you the finest Arabica and Robusta beans.

From hand-pounding spices in our kitchen to slow-brewing every cup in brass filters, we preserve the traditional "Kaapi" ritual while embracing modern roastery techniques.
